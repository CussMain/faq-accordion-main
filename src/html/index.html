FAQs
What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?
Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.
Is Frontend Mentor free?
Yes, Frontend Mentor offers a free plan that gives you access to a limited number of challenges. You can also subscribe to a paid plan for more features and benefits.
Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?
Absolutely! Frontend Mentor encourages you to use your completed projects to showcase your skills to potential employers or clients.
How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?
Frontend Mentor has a vibrant community forum where you can ask questions, seek feedback, and collaborate with other developers.
